Website Plan: Dulce Aroma Bakery

Site Name

Proposed Site Name: Dulce Aroma Bakery

The name “Dulce Aroma Bakery” reflects the warm, inviting feeling of a local shop where fresh bread and pastries are baked daily.

Optional domain: dulcearoma-bakery.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Dulce Aroma Bakery website is to introduce visitors to the bakery, display its products, and provide essential information such as hours and location.

  • Showcase bread, pastries, and seasonal baked goods.
  • Present the bakery’s story.
  • Provide a photo gallery.
  • Show hours, address, and a simple contact form.
  • Use semantic HTML, responsive CSS, and JavaScript.

Target Audience Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: “What types of bread and pastries does this bakery offer?”
  • Scenario 2: “Where is the bakery located and what are the operating hours?”
  • Scenario 3: “Can I view images of the products before visiting?”

Color Scheme

The color palette is inspired by artisan bakery tones: dark wood, warm cream, and golden accents.

Usage: Headings, header, primary buttons.

Secondary Color

Usage: Highlights, borders, accents.

Neutral Background

Hex: #F6E9D7
